About Us

Building the future of AI
from Africa for the world

Okapi AI was founded with a vision to make advanced technology accessible, practical, and beneficial for everyone.

Our founder first discovered the transformative potential of Artificial Intelligence while studying at the University of Johannesburg. Inspired by the opportunities AI could create for individuals, businesses, and society, the company began as an informal venture in 2025, providing AI and technology training to high school students in Johannesburg.

As demand for intelligent solutions continued to grow, Okapi AI was officially registered in South Africa in January 2026 (Registration No. 2026/032715/07). Since then, the company has evolved from a training initiative into a technology partner focused on helping organizations harness the power of data, artificial intelligence, automation, and security.

Today, we are building one of the most trusted technology partners for people and organizations across Africa and beyond. Our mission is to deliver superior intelligence that enables better decision-making, drives innovation, improves productivity, and contributes to a safer and more prosperous future.

How we work

Field-Driven Engineering


At Okapi AI, we operate using a Field-Driven Engineering (FDE) model — inspired by modern AI-native companies that embed themselves directly into real-world environments to build solutions that matter.

FDE is our way of ensuring that every product, system, and solution is built from real problems, real users, and real environments — not assumptions.


01

Field Immersion

We embed ourselves in the environments we serve — logistics, finance, education, government, and enterprises — to deeply understand workflows, constraints, and pain points.

02

Data-First Discovery

We collect and structure real operational data before building solutions, ensuring every AI system is grounded in truth, not assumptions.

03

Engineering in Context

Our AI, automation, and security systems are built directly around how users actually work in the field, not how we think they should work.

04

Rapid Iteration Loops

We continuously deploy, test, and refine solutions in live environments to ensure performance, reliability, and impact.

05

Embedded Intelligence

We don't just build tools — we integrate intelligence into the core of business operations, making systems smarter over time.
